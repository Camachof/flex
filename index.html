What is an L-system?

The Dragon, Hilbert, Sierpinski and Koch curves are produced by only drawing forward and turning left or right by a particular angle.

Curves are wild beasts that inhabit the math world. Try changing the number of iterationis to experiment, but beware! Anything past 10 is bound to be too big or too impredictable to intellegbly render on screen.
